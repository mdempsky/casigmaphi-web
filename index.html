Why Parliamentary Procedure?

Rules of Order

Fair, objective rules give all members equal opportunity to participate.

Orderly Meetings

The presiding officer keeps meetings on topic and on schedule.

Effective Debate

Balance the majority's right to decide with the minority's right to be heard.

Clear Decisions

Center discussion on yes/no questions with clear outcomes.
